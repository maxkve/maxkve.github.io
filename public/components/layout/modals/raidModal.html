Raids
Standard Procedure is a PvE-oriented guild, and raids represent the pinnacle of the Classic PvE experience. We raid because we think it's the most enjoyable part of the game, and we want to experience it all if possible. As we're mostly older and have less time on our hands at this point in our lives, being efficient while raiding becomes very important. We expect members to respect eachother by showing up on time, and focusing during raids. We raid 2 times per week, with 1 slot open for an extra raid if need be.

RAID TIMES

  • Sundays 18.30-22.00
  • Tuesdays (extra) 18.30-22.00
Raid times are in GMT+1.
