Organization
Standard Procedure is an international semi-casual guild on Noggenfogger-EU. We want everyone to have a good time in Azeroth together - conquering raids and other challenges along the way. Our goal is to clear all the PvE content the game has to offer, at our own pace. We believe that the one key factor in reaching our goals is Respect.
  • Treat guild members and other players with courtesy and respect and good things will follow. Remember that you're representing each and every individual in the guild when you're out in Azeroth. Don't make them look bad by association, make them look good!
  • As we're mostly older and have less time on our hands at this point in our lives, being efficient while raiding becomes very important. We expect members to respect eachother by showing up on time and focusing during raids. Outside the instance ready to go at raid start!
